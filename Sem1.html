Engineering Mechanics

Credits 6
BOOK M.D. Dayal
DIFFICULTY Medium
  1. Tutions are preferrable since you are introduced to this subject for the first time and concepts need to be given time to understand.
  2. There's no limit to problems in Mechanics.
  3. The best book to buy is 'Engineering Mechanics by M.D. Dayal'.
  4. This subject carries 6 credits. Your pointer will be affected if you don't score well.
  5. It'll be difficult for you to clear it if you get a K.T. in the subject. Clear it in your very first attempt.
  6. Dynamics is more difficult than Statics. In dynamics, you need to think about the situation of the question, choose the correct formulae and solve.
  7. Statics is comparatively easy because the basic thing is to equate the sum of all forces to the resultant force to solve the problem. You don't need to think much.
  8. You must've done Friction in earlier standards and this makes statics easier.
