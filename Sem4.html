Operating Systems

Includes Lab Practicals
Credits 4
BOOK William Stallings
Galvin
DIFFICULTY Medium

  • This is one of the core subjects for placements.
  • Not too difficult it teaches you things about OS you probably never knew before on Linux OS.
  • Classes are not necessary at all, because the subject is self explanatory.
  • As a computer enginner you should be aware how OSs work and so you cannot take this subject lightly at all.
  • Techmax is the best for this subject, but Galvin is even better with all concepts properly explained.
  • You will have a mini project for OS at the end of the semester, choose your topic such that it is not too time consuming yet not too simple, you maybe asked about it in placement interviews.
